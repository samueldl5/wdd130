Down the River

Down to a fun experience! Here at Down the River our purpose is to take you down, you guessed it, one of our most acclaimed rivers! With qualified guides and the proper equipment, we guarantee you a refreshing, safe and, of course, fun experience for you and your family!

History

Down the River was founded in 1987 when two young brothers, after 3 years of diligent work, formally organized the company to further agilize their efforts and reach, with an eye single in delivering as many people as possible the joyful experience this sport rightfully has to offer.
